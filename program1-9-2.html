Poster presentations by master/PhD students in Xi’an

List of Contributions

A special session on Wednesday morning will be devoted to poster presentations by students doing their master/PhD in some Universities in China, and working on topics related to the WODES themes.

 

The purpose of the session is to provide an opportunity for young researchers in these Universities in the field of Discrete Event Systems and related areas, to present their work and interact with the WODES participants.

 

The interested students should send an email to Prof. Carla Seatzu (seatzu@diee.unica.it) with the following information:

  • the title of the presentation,
  • the name of persons involved in the research and their affiliations
  • an abstract (approximately one page) describing the content of the presentation

The deadline for the submission is May 20th, 2016.

 

The Program Committee will select among the proposed contributions based on the interest of theme for the WODES community and the quality of presentation of the proposal.

 

No registration fee is due by students presenting a poster.
